grav

You're in a rocket in space.

Rocket always looks at mouse.
Left-click to fire engines, right-click to fire retro-engines.
Explore around, don't crash, don't fly into suns!
Spaceports refuel and repair your ship.

(Find the big ginormous sun! All the way north.)

Made in <48 hours at the Fall 2014 Game Jam organised by the UWaterloo Games Institute.
